Colornames My own image

Colornames is a website that aims to give every colour that exists in sRGB colour space a natural name.

It is community-driven, and anyone on the internet can vote for and give names for colours. So far, 3787228 colours have been named. That's 22.6% percent of all sRGB colours.¹
