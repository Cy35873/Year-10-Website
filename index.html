Cool math stuff I discovered

3D prism of \(s\) sides

First, define the perameter variables

\[0\le R_1<\tau\] \[0\le R_2<\tau\] \[s\in\mathbb Z^+\] \[\Lambda=[0\cdots S]\] \[h\in\mathbb R\]

Then we can use a big list of points from our perameters

\[B=\left(\cos\left(R_1+\frac\tau s\Lambda\right),\sin\left(R_1+\frac\tau s\Lambda\right)\cos R_2\right)\] \[T=\left(B.x,B.y+h\sin R_2\right)\]

And join the points up to make lines

\[\operatorname{polygon}\left(B\right)\] \[\operatorname{polygon}\left(T\right)\] \[\operatorname{polygon}\left(B,T\right)\]

Try it out

Create \(\arcsin x\) from scratch

\[\int_0^x\frac1{\sqrt{1-t^2}}dt=\arcsin x\]

Also, \(\frac1{\arcsin'x}=\sqrt{1-x^2}\)

Create \(\arctan x\) from scratch

\[\int_0^x\frac1{t^2+1}dt=\arctan x\]

Also, \(\frac1{\arctan'x}-1=x^2\)

Calculate \(\pi\) using an integral

\[4\int_0^1\frac1{x^2+1}dx=\pi\] \[\int\frac1{x^2+1}dx=\arctan x+C\] \[\therefore4\int_0^1\arctan x\,dx=\pi\]

The thingy I discovered to help evaluate \(\lim_{x\to n}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\)

\[\lim_{x\to n}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\lim_{x\to n}\frac{xf'(n)+f(n)}{xg'(n)+g(n)}\] \[\text{For exmaple}\quad\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\sin x}x=\lim_{x\to0}\frac{x\sin'x}{x\frac d{dx}}=\lim_{x\to0}\frac xx=1\]
